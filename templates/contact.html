About us

Farm-To-Table is founded in 2024 with the goal of connecting local farms to consumers through technology. A small team is employed to develop a software platform that streamlines the farm-to-table process by managing orders, deliveries, and inventory for small farmers. The mission is focused on supporting sustainable agriculture and providing fresh, local produce to consumers, while communities are empowered through innovative solutions.
